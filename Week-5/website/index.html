Worlds Reknown University.

Plans for the establishment of JKUAT started in 1977. In early 1978, the Kenyan president, Jomo Kenyatta,
donated 200 hectares of farmland for the establishment of the university. The first group of students were admitted on 4 May 1981.
The new president Daniel Arap Moi formally opened JKUAT on 17 March 1982.
